hackathons are fun. but let's face it, there's always that one group member that never does any work.

they code for half an hour, say they "locked in", and then take a 4 hour break to play brawl stars.

what if you gave them a timer that included breaks?

hackmato is a pomodoro timer for your next hackathon, with breaks already started at 5 minutes (the length of a brawl stars match!).

you can change the working time, short break time, long break time, and how many short breaks before a long break.
